Bidding & Budget Strategy

Bidding Strategy

Start with Maximize Conversions. Switch to Target CPA after accumulating 30+ conversions. This gives Google enough data to optimize.

Daily Budget

Start at $20-30/day per campaign. Monitor for 2 weeks, then scale the winning campaigns and pause underperformers.

Value-Based Bidding

Assign conversion values: Consultation booking = $500, Form fill = $50, Phone call = $200. This trains Google to prioritize high-value leads.

Ad Schedule

Mon-Fri, 8am-8pm (business hours). B2B decision makers search during work. Reduce or pause weekend spend initially.

Target Locations

Start with India + US + UK + UAE (English-speaking markets with high-ticket potential). Expand based on performance data.

Devices

Target all devices, but monitor mobile vs desktop conversion rates. B2B tends to convert better on desktop — adjust bids accordingly.

Google Ads Setup Checklist

Before Launching

  • Set up conversion tracking: form submissions, phone calls, and consultation bookings
  • Link Google Analytics 4 to your Google Ads account
  • Add the Google Ads tag to your website (via GTM or direct)
  • Set up Enhanced Conversions for better attribution
  • Create separate ad groups within each campaign for keyword themes
  • Add all negative keywords at the account level
  • Enable call extensions with your phone number: +91 6309821905
  • Add sitelink extensions: Services, About, Contact, Blog
  • Add callout extensions: Free Consultation, Results in 90 Days, India-Based
  • Add structured snippet extensions: Services: AI Automation, Web Dev, AEO, Marketing
  • Ensure each landing page has a clear CTA above the fold
  • Test landing page load speed (aim for under 3 seconds)

After First 2 Weeks

  • Review Search Terms Report — add irrelevant terms as negative keywords
  • Check Quality Scores — aim for 7+ on all keywords
  • Replace "Low" performing headlines/descriptions with new variations
  • Identify top-performing ad combinations and create dedicated ad groups for them
  • Analyze which campaigns generate actual leads (not just clicks)
  • Scale budget on winning campaigns, pause losing ones
  • Set up remarketing audiences for website visitors who didn't convert

Key Metrics to Track

  • Cost Per Lead: target under $15-25 (India), $50-80 (US/UK)
  • Click-Through Rate: target 5%+ (search average is 6.66%)
  • Conversion Rate: target 5-10% on landing pages
  • Quality Score: target 7+ per keyword
  • Ad Strength: target "Good" or "Excellent"
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): track revenue generated vs ad spend
